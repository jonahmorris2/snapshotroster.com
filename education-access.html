Snapshot Education Access Program

Free licenses for qualifying schools and teachers in underserved communities.

Apply for Education Access

Supporting educators with practical classroom tools.

Snapshot Roster System aims to support teachers and schools that need organized classroom software but may not have access to large software budgets. Submitting an application does not guarantee license approval.

Open Application Form

Who it is for

Teachers, small schools, ESL programs, and education centers that need practical classroom workflow support.

What it may include

Access to Snapshot licenses, classroom workflow tools, and local-first classroom management features.

Important note

Applications are reviewed individually. Availability, eligibility, and approval are not guaranteed.
