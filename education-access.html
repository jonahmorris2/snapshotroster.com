Education access initiative

Practical classroom tools should not only belong to well-funded schools.

The Snapshot Education Access Program is intended to provide free licenses for qualifying schools and teachers in underserved communities.

Apply for Education Access

Mission-focused software access

Snapshot is being built as practical teacher software. This program is a way to support educators who may benefit from classroom management tools but may not have access to large software budgets.

Who the program is for.

The program is designed with real classrooms, small programs, and resource limitations in mind.

🏫

Schools

Small schools, academies, and education programs that need practical classroom organization tools.

👩‍🏫

Teachers

Teachers who personally manage attendance, grading, seating charts, and student records.

🌍

Underserved communities

Communities where access to paid classroom software may be limited by budget, infrastructure, or availability.

Request access through the application form.

The application helps determine whether a school, teacher, or program may qualify for the Snapshot Education Access Program.
