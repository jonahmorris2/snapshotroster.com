Start with the Lite version

Snapshot Lite is designed for teachers who want a low-friction way to explore the Snapshot classroom workflow. It is a starting point for trying the system before moving to a more complete version later.

Free to try

Snapshot Lite gives teachers a simple way to experience the product without needing a paid license first.

Built for teachers

The system is designed around daily classroom tasks such as rosters, attendance, schedules, seating charts, and student organization.

Local-first approach

Snapshot is designed as desktop classroom software with a focus on local control, practical workflow, and offline access.

Current compatibility

  • Windows 10 compatible
  • Windows 11 compatible
  • Desktop installer-based setup
  • Designed for local classroom use

Future platform goals

  • Android compatibility is a future goal
  • Linux compatibility is a future goal
  • Portable data support helps prepare for future platform expansion
  • Future availability is not guaranteed and depends on development progress

How to get started

  1. Click the Download Snapshot Lite button.
  2. Save the installer to your Windows 10 or Windows 11 computer.
  3. Open the installer and follow the setup instructions.
  4. Launch Snapshot Roster System.
  5. Begin exploring the classroom workflow and available Lite features.
